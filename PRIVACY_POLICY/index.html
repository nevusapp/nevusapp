Privacy Policy for Nevus

Last Updated: March 15, 2026

Introduction

Nevus ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mole tracking application.

Our Core Principle: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.

Information We Collect

Data You Create

When you use Nevus, you create the following data stored locally on your device:

  • Photos: Images of moles and body regions you capture
  • Metadata: Date, time, and technical information about photos
  • Notes: Text notes you add to moles or overview images
  • Organization Data: Body region, body side, and mole categorization
  • Reference Selections: Which images you've marked as reference images

Data We Do NOT Collect

We do NOT collect, access, or transmit:

  • ❌ Personal identification information
  • ❌ Location data (GPS coordinates)
  • ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
  • ❌ Device identifiers
  • ❌ Contact information
  • ❌ Health data beyond what you create in the app
  • ❌ Crash reports or diagnostics (unless you explicitly share them)

How We Use Your Information

Local Storage Only

All data you create in Nevus is:

  • ✅ Stored locally on your device in the app's secure container
  • ✅ Protected by iOS file encryption
  • ✅ Never transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
  • ✅ Never shared with third parties
  • ✅ Never used for advertising or marketing
  • ✅ Never analyzed or processed by us

iOS Device Backup

Your Nevus data is automatically included in your standard iOS device backup:

  • iCloud Backup: If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device
  • Computer Backup: When you backup via iTunes or Finder
  • Encryption: Protected by Apple's encryption standards
  • Control: You control backup settings through iOS Settings

Important: This is standard iOS backup functionality. We do not have access to your iCloud account or backups.

Data Sharing

Device-to-Device Sync (AirDrop)

When you use the sync feature:

  • Data is transferred directly between your devices via AirDrop
  • Transfer is encrypted by iOS
  • No data passes through the internet or our servers
  • You control what data to export and when
  • You choose which device receives the data

Export Features

When you export data:

  • ZIP Export: Creates a file on your device that you can share
  • Image Export: Shares individual images via iOS Share Sheet
  • Your Control: You decide where to send exported data
  • No Tracking: We don't know what you do with exported files

No Third-Party Sharing

We do NOT share your data with:

  • ❌ Analytics services
  • ❌ Advertising networks
  • ❌ Cloud storage providers (beyond standard iOS backup)
  • ❌ Social media platforms
  • ❌ Medical institutions (unless you explicitly share)
  • ❌ Any other third parties

Data Security

Protection Measures

Your data is protected by:

  • iOS File Encryption: All app data is encrypted by iOS
  • App Sandbox: Data isolated from other apps
  • Secure Container: App-specific storage protected by iOS
  • No Network Transmission: Data never leaves your device (except via AirDrop when you initiate it)

Your Responsibilities

To keep your data secure:

  • Use a strong device passcode
  • Enable Face ID or Touch ID
  • Keep your iOS updated
  • Enable device encryption (iOS does this by default)
  • Only share data with trusted recipients

Your Rights and Choices

Data Access

You have complete access to all your data:

  • View all photos and notes in the app
  • Export data at any time
  • No restrictions or limitations

Data Deletion

You have complete control to delete your data:

  • Delete individual photos by swiping left
  • Delete entire moles from the detail view
  • Delete overview images
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

Note: Deleted data cannot be recovered unless you have a device backup.

Data Portability

You can export your data at any time:

  • Export individual moles as ZIP files
  • Export all data as a complete backup
  • Export individual images as JPEG files
  • Data includes JSON metadata for portability

Children's Privacy

Nevus is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has used the app, please contact us.

Medical Disclaimer

Important: Nevus is a tracking tool, not a medical device.

  • ❌ Not intended for diagnosis or treatment
  • ❌ Not a substitute for professional medical advice
  • ❌ Not approved by medical regulatory authorities
  • ✅ For personal tracking and organization only
  • ✅ Always consult healthcare professionals for medical concerns

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.

How you'll be notified: - In-app notification for significant changes - Updated policy available in the app and on our website - Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of changes

International Users

GDPR Compliance (European Union)

For users in the EU:

  • Data Controller: You are the controller of your data
  • Data Processing: All processing happens locally on your device
  • Right to Access: You have full access to your data
  • Right to Deletion: You can delete data at any time
  • Right to Portability: You can export data in standard formats
  • No Profiling: We don't profile or make automated decisions

CCPA Compliance (California)

For California residents:

  • No Sale of Data: We don't sell your personal information
  • No Sharing: We don't share your data with third parties
  • Access Rights: You have full access to your data
  • Deletion Rights: You can delete your data at any time

Technical Details

Data Storage

  • Format: SwiftData (Apple's data framework)
  • Location: App's secure container on your device
  • Encryption: iOS file-level encryption
  • Backup: Included in standard iOS device backup

Network Usage

The app uses network connectivity only for:

  • ❌ NOT for data transmission to servers
  • ❌ NOT for analytics or tracking
  • ✅ AirDrop transfers (local WiFi/Bluetooth, when you initiate)
  • ✅ App Store updates (handled by iOS)

Permissions

The app requests these iOS permissions:

  • Camera: To capture photos of moles (required)
  • Notifications: For monthly reminders (optional)
  • Files: To save exported data (optional)

We only use these permissions for their stated purposes.

Contact Information

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please open a github issue.

Transparency Commitment

We believe in complete transparency:

  • ✅ No hidden data collection
  • ✅ No tracking or analytics
  • ✅ No third-party services
  • ✅ No advertising networks
  • ✅ No cloud storage (beyond standard iOS backup)
  • ✅ Open about what we do and don't do

Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR)

For EU users, our legal basis for processing is:

  • Consent: You choose to use the app and create data
  • Legitimate Interest: Providing the app's functionality
  • Contract: Fulfilling our agreement to provide the service

Data Retention

  • Active Use: Data remains on your device as long as you use the app
  • After Deletion: Data is immediately removed from the app
  • Backups: May remain in iOS device backups until you delete them
  • No Server Storage: We don't retain any data on servers

Third-Party Services

Nevus does NOT use any third-party services, including:

  • ❌ Analytics (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
  • ❌ Crash reporting (Crashlytics, Sentry, etc.)
  • ❌ Cloud storage (AWS, Google Cloud, etc.)
  • ❌ Advertising (AdMob, Facebook Ads, etc.)
  • ❌ Social media SDKs

The only third-party involvement is:

  • ✅ Apple's iOS (for device functionality)
  • ✅ App Store (for distribution)

Your Privacy is Our Priority

We built Nevus with privacy as the foundation, not an afterthought. Your health data is sensitive, and we believe you should have complete control over it.

Questions? We're here to help. Contact us anytime.
