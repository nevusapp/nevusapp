# Privacy Policy for Nevus

**Last Updated: March 15, 2026**

## Introduction

Nevus ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use our mole tracking application.

**Our Core Principle: Your data stays on your device. We don't collect, transmit, or store any of your personal information on our servers.**

---

## Information We Collect

### Data You Create

When you use Nevus, you create the following data **stored locally on your device**:

- **Photos**: Images of moles and body regions you capture
- **Metadata**: Date, time, and technical information about photos
- **Notes**: Text notes you add to moles or overview images
- **Organization Data**: Body region, body side, and mole categorization
- **Reference Selections**: Which images you've marked as reference images

### Data We Do NOT Collect

We do **NOT** collect, access, or transmit:

- ❌ Personal identification information
- ❌ Location data (GPS coordinates)
- ❌ Usage analytics or statistics
- ❌ Device identifiers
- ❌ Contact information
- ❌ Health data beyond what you create in the app
- ❌ Crash reports or diagnostics (unless you explicitly share them)

---

## How We Use Your Information

### Local Storage Only

All data you create in Nevus is:

- ✅ Stored **locally** on your device in the app's secure container
- ✅ Protected by iOS file encryption
- ✅ Never transmitted to our servers (we don't have servers)
- ✅ Never shared with third parties
- ✅ Never used for advertising or marketing
- ✅ Never analyzed or processed by us

### iOS Device Backup

Your Nevus data is automatically included in your standard iOS device backup:

- **iCloud Backup**: If you have iCloud Backup enabled on your device
- **Computer Backup**: When you backup via iTunes or Finder
- **Encryption**: Protected by Apple's encryption standards
- **Control**: You control backup settings through iOS Settings

**Important**: This is standard iOS backup functionality. We do not have access to your iCloud account or backups.

---

## Data Sharing

### Device-to-Device Sync (AirDrop)

When you use the sync feature:

- Data is transferred **directly** between your devices via AirDrop
- Transfer is **encrypted** by iOS
- No data passes through the internet or our servers
- You control what data to export and when
- You choose which device receives the data

### Export Features

When you export data:

- **ZIP Export**: Creates a file on your device that you can share
- **Image Export**: Shares individual images via iOS Share Sheet
- **Your Control**: You decide where to send exported data
- **No Tracking**: We don't know what you do with exported files

### No Third-Party Sharing

We do **NOT** share your data with:

- ❌ Analytics services
- ❌ Advertising networks
- ❌ Cloud storage providers (beyond standard iOS backup)
- ❌ Social media platforms
- ❌ Medical institutions (unless you explicitly share)
- ❌ Any other third parties

---

## Data Security

### Protection Measures

Your data is protected by:

- **iOS File Encryption**: All app data is encrypted by iOS
- **App Sandbox**: Data isolated from other apps
- **Secure Container**: App-specific storage protected by iOS
- **No Network Transmission**: Data never leaves your device (except via AirDrop when you initiate it)

### Your Responsibilities

To keep your data secure:

- Use a strong device passcode
- Enable Face ID or Touch ID
- Keep your iOS updated
- Enable device encryption (iOS does this by default)
- Only share data with trusted recipients

---

## Your Rights and Choices

### Data Access

You have complete access to all your data:

- View all photos and notes in the app
- Export data at any time
- No restrictions or limitations

### Data Deletion

You have complete control to delete your data:

- Delete individual photos by swiping left
- Delete entire moles from the detail view
- Delete overview images
- Uninstall the app to remove all data

**Note**: Deleted data cannot be recovered unless you have a device backup.

### Data Portability

You can export your data at any time:

- Export individual moles as ZIP files
- Export all data as a complete backup
- Export individual images as JPEG files
- Data includes JSON metadata for portability
